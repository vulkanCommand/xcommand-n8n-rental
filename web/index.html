About xCommand Cloud
What we’re building and why it matters

xCommand Cloud is a hosted automation layer built by Durga Kalyan & Akshay Suryawanshi for people who want powerful workflows without babysitting infrastructure. You get your own n8n workspace, on our hardware, tuned for reliability and speed.

The idea is simple: make serious automation and AI workflows accessible to everyone with zero setup and zero maintenance. Our goal is to become the place where operators, founders, and teams can experiment, ship, and scale workflows without waiting on internal IT or heavyweight tools.

Mission: remove friction from automation
Vision: advanced tools, one click away
  • Self-contained workspaces: each user gets an isolated environment with their own flows, credentials, and data.
  • Built for experiments and live ops: perfect for weekend projects, MVPs, or running small internal tools.
  • Transparent pricing: short, focused plans (24 hours or 5 days) so you only pay for what you actually use.

How it works
From payment to first workflow in three steps
01
Pick a plan

Choose between a 24-hour or 5-day workspace on the plans page. You’ll get instant confirmation after payment.

02
Get your workspace link

We generate a unique URL and credentials for your n8n instance. If you already subscribed, just head to Open my workspace.

03
Build and export

Build workflows, test them with your tools and APIs, and download your flows as JSON before the workspace expires so you can reuse them anywhere.

Your automations stay yours
Export before expiry, reuse wherever you want

Every workflow you build in xCommand Cloud is fully exportable. Before your plan ends, you can download your automation logic as n8n JSON and import it into any other n8n instance you control.

That means xCommand can act as your fast sandbox, your MVP lab, or the bridge environment you use while your long-term infrastructure catches up.

Export promise

You can download your flows as JSON at any time before expiry or by the end of your plan. No lock-in, no hidden traps.

We treat your workspace as your space. Our job is to host it, not hold it hostage.
